Why Join?

Wetlands Park Friends is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that provides flexible support for Clark County Wetlands Park’s programs, activities, and ongoing needs.

Your membership helps support student field trip transportation, volunteer stewardship supplies, educational materials, special event support, and Park enhancement projects.

Other ways to join or renew

Prefer to join or renew by cash or check?

Wetlands Park Friends accepts cash or check memberships at the Wetlands Park Nature Center, open Tuesday – Sunday, 9am – 3pm.

For membership questions, contact Wetlands Park Friends at: friends@wetlandsparkfriends.org

About the Park

About Clark County Wetlands Park

Clark County Wetlands Park is a natural escape in the Las Vegas Valley, offering trails, wildlife viewing, educational programs, and opportunities to connect with the unique wetlands habitat. Wetlands Park Friends is an all-volunteer 501(c)(3) nonprofit that supports the Park’s programs, activities, and special projects.

Programs & Experiences

Connecting People with Nature

Clark County Wetlands Park is one of the Las Vegas Valley’s hidden gems — a place where visitors can step away from the city, explore trails, watch wildlife, and experience the natural world close to home.

Educational Programs

Park programs help children, families, and adults learn about wetlands habitat, wildlife, conservation, and the importance of protecting natural spaces.

Student Field Trips

For many students, a field trip to Wetlands Park may be one of their first opportunities to explore trails, see wildlife, and experience outdoor learning beyond the classroom.

Community Connection

Wetlands Park Friends helps support the programs, supplies, transportation, special events, and visitor experiences that make the Park more welcoming, accessible, and engaging for the community.
